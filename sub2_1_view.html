Colored arrangements (Blanc) Colored arrangements (Blanc) Colored arrangements (Blanc)

for formal celebrations such as store openings and office relocations
판매가

제품정보
A refreshing arrangement of white and green flowers.
The flowers are inserted into absorbent sponges, so you can enjoy displaying them as they are.
It uses orchids such as Dendrobium and Cymbidium, and has a long shelf life and an elegant impression.
White and green arrangements, which evoke the image of a new beginning, are popular colors for corporate celebrations such as IPOs, inaugurations, and relocations.
*Paid optional items (premium cards, birthday picks, paper bags) cannot be used.
